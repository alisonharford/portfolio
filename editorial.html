JERK Magazine, Art Direction

Role

Editorial Design
Art Direction
Graphic Illustrations

Programs: Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Lightroom

As Art Director at JERK Magazine, I worked with illustrators and photographers to create the design of the magazine. Each spread had to match the issue's theme. I worked on two issues: The Protest Issue, which featured big, bold type; and The Zine Issue, which featured collage-like art created by me "taped" to the magazine. Below are spreads from each issue. The Protest Issue won first place in the Design category of the 2020 Association for Education in Journalism and Mass Communication Student Magazine contest, and won two College Media Pinnacle awards.
