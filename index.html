Resume

I'm a multimedia journalist currently based in Moab, Utah. Here, I work as the art director of and reporter for the Moab Sun News, an award-winning local newspaper. I'm passionate about utilizing every platform—writing, photography, print design, web design, social media, newsletters, and data visualization—to engage an audience and tell a story.

Recently, I designed coverage for a 100-year flood that hit Moab: I reported on and wrote four stories to cover the flood from every angle, took photos, designed the print paper and created a cohesive flow on our website and across social media. View it online, or view the print edition.

I'm also earning a Master's Certificate in Data Visualization and Infographics from Parsons School of Design. Check out what I'm working on.
