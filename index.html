About

I'm a senior at Syracuse University with a dual major in Magazine Journalism and Geography — my studies are focused on how culture is developed by interacting with landscape, and how to tell that everchanging story. I'm absolutely obsessed with maps, but I'm even more obsessed with lemon cake, terrible fiction novels, and the Rocky Mountains. I've worked for The Daily Orange, National Geographic, environmental education summer camps, environmental centers, and for various other places, including a rec center in New Zealand.
