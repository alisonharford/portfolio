Conservation Research Poster

Role

Research
Illustration
Body Copy
Design
Data Visualization

Programs: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign, QGIS, Google Earth, RawGraphs.io, Microsoft Excel

This poster was the final product of a semester’s worth of research in the field, running an experiments in university glasshouses, and doing copious reading. I went through many versions of the graphic in the middle, which shows concentrations of plant species present on the property in eight different sites.
I ultimately decided the circular graph was the most aesthetically pleasing and allowed the viewer to place themselves topographically on the property I was presenting on. It also allows the viewer to group plant species by altitude, which was an interesting scientific observation.
